Charge Credit Card Immediately

The integration service allows to charge a credit card in a one step process without initiating a separate authorization before.

Note: it is necessary to do a creditcardcheck via PAYONE client API to avoid complying to PCI-DSS standard.

Examples

Successful Charge

If I charge a credit card with an amount without authorizing it before, an interfaceInteraction is added to the payment: and after that, an "authorization" request to PAYONE will be sent. The PAYONE response will lead to changes in the interfaceInteraction:

Pending Charge

If I charge a credit card with an amount without authorizing it before, an interfaceInteraction is added to the payment: and after that, an "authorization" request to PAYONE will be sent with status PENDING. No changes to the interfaceInteraction will be made.
